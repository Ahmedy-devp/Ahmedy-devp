Each year, we search the globe for inspirational members to represent World Gym. Members on 6 continents enter for the chance to become ambassadors by posting workout photos on social media with the hashtag #WorldGymAmbassador. The winners star in a series of workout videos offering advice and motivation to help everyone achieve their fitness goals.
